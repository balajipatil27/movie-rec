Wolfwalkers released:2020
"Wolfwalkers" is a 2020 animated fantasy film directed by Tomm Moore and Ross Stewart, produced by Cartoon Saloon, and distributed by Apple TV+. Set in a magical version of 17th century Ireland, the film follows the story of Robyn, a young English girl who moves to Kilkenny with her father, a wolf hunter. Robyn befriends Mebh, a free-spirited girl who is part of a tribe rumored to have the ability to transform into wolves while asleep, known as Wolfwalkers. As Robyn becomes entangled in Mebh's world, she discovers the importance of friendship, empathy, and protecting nature. "Wolfwalkers" received critical acclaim for its stunning animation, rich storytelling, and themes of environmentalism and cultural identity.

Ninja Scroll released : 1993
"Ninja Scroll" is a Japanese animated film released in 1993, directed by Yoshiaki Kawajiri and produced by Madhouse studio. Set in feudal Japan, the story follows Jubei Kibagami, a skilled ninja swordsman who becomes embroiled in a plot involving a powerful group of ninjas known as the Eight Devils of Kimon. After being poisoned by one of the Devils, Jubei joins forces with a female ninja named Kagero and a wandering swordsman named Dakuan to stop the sinister group's plans. Filled with intense action sequences, supernatural elements, and mature themes, "Ninja Scroll" is celebrated for its stylish animation, dynamic fight scenes, and dark atmosphere. It is considered a classic of Japanese animation and a cornerstone of the anime genre.

Nimona released :2023
ANIMAL
"Nimona" is a 2023 animated fantasy film directed by Patrick Osborne and based on the webcomic of the same name by Noelle Stevenson. The story is set in a medieval-futuristic world where magic and technology coexist. It follows the adventures of Nimona, a shape-shifting young girl who becomes the sidekick of the villainous Lord Ballister Blackheart. Together, they challenge the authority of the Institution, a powerful organization led by the ruthless Sir Ambrosius Goldenloin. As Nimona and Blackheart uncover dark secrets about the Institution, they must confront their own loyalties and the true nature of heroism. "Nimona" received praise for its unique animation style, engaging characters, and themes of friendship, identity, and redemption.
