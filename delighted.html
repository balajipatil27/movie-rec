Spider-man: into the spider verse
Marvel
"Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse" is a 2018 animated film that introduces the concept of the multiverse within the Spider-Man universe. It follows teenager Miles Morales as he gains spider-like abilities and teams up with alternate versions of Spider-Man to stop the villainous Kingpin from destroying reality. The film's innovative animation and diverse cast earned critical acclaim and an Academy Award for Best Animated Feature.

Trip to the moon
"Trip to the Moon" is a classic 1902 silent film directed by Georges Méliès. It depicts a group of astronomers who embark on a fantastical journey to the moon using a cannon-propelled spaceship. Upon arrival, they encounter strange lunar inhabitants, including the iconic "man in the moon." The film is renowned for its groundbreaking special effects and imaginative storytelling, establishing Méliès as a pioneer of early cinema.

Thor : Rangnarok
marvel
"Thor: Ragnarok" is a 2017 Marvel superhero film directed by Taika Waititi. It follows Thor, the Norse god of thunder, as he finds himself imprisoned on the other side of the universe without his mighty hammer. In a race against time to prevent the apocalyptic event known as Ragnarok, Thor must escape captivity and confront his ruthless sister, Hela, who seeks to destroy his home world of Asgard. Along the way, he forms an unlikely alliance with his former ally, the Hulk, and a band of misfit warriors. Filled with humor, action, and stunning visuals, "Thor: Ragnarok" is celebrated for its refreshing take on the superhero genre and its vibrant, colorful aesthetic.

"Everything Everywhere All at Once" is a 2022 science fiction film directed by Daniel Kwan and Daniel Scheinert. It follows the story of a Chinese-American woman named Evelyn Wang, played by Michelle Yeoh, who discovers she can access parallel universes. As she navigates her various identities across these alternate realities, she becomes entangled in a cosmic battle to save the multiverse. The film blends elements of action, comedy, drama, and existential exploration, offering a unique and mind-bending cinematic experience.?
