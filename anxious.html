Bollywood
"Yaariyan 2" is a Bollywood film that serves as a sequel to the 2014 movie "Yaariyan." It continues the story of friendship, love, and youthful exuberance set against the backdrop of college life. Happy Days
Telugu
"Happy Days" is a Telugu-language coming-of-age film released in 2007, directed by Sekhar Kammula. Set on a college campus, the movie revolves around a group of engineering students and their experiences, friendships, and romantic relationships. The film captures the essence of college life, with its blend of fun, camaraderie, challenges, and aspirations. It explores themes of friendship, love, heartbreak, and the pursuit of dreams. With its youthful energy, memorable characters, and catchy music, "Happy Days" became a commercial success and a beloved cult classic among Telugu cinema audiences.

Life is beautiful
Hollywood
"Life is Beautiful" is a 1997 Italian film directed by and starring Roberto Benigni. Set during the Holocaust, it tells the story of a Jewish Italian man named Guido who uses his humor and imagination to shield his son from the horrors of their internment in a Nazi concentration camp. Guido creates an elaborate game to convince his son that their situation is merely a contest with the grand prize being a tank. Through his love and optimism, Guido manages to maintain hope and preserve the innocence of his child amidst the darkest of circumstances. The film is celebrated for its poignant portrayal of the human spirit's resilience in the face of adversity and won three Academy Awards, including Best Foreign Language Film.

Kumbalangi Nights
Malayalam
"Kumbalangi Nights" is a 2019 Malayalam-language film directed by Madhu C. Narayanan. Set in the picturesque village of Kumbalangi in Kerala, India, the movie revolves around four brothers dealing with various personal struggles and conflicts. The narrative delves into themes of family dynamics, masculinity, love, and redemption. It offers a realistic portrayal of life in a coastal village while exploring the complexities of human relationships. "Kumbalangi Nights" received widespread critical acclaim for its brilliant performances, engaging storytelling, and nuanced character development, cementing its status as one of the most acclaimed Indian films of recent years.
